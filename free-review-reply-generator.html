Tips for Better Review Responses

  • Reply within 24-48 hours. Speed matters. Customers check back to see if you responded, and Google rewards timely engagement.
  • Reference specific details. Mentioning something the reviewer said ("glad you enjoyed the carbonara") proves you actually read their review.
  • Keep it concise. 3-5 sentences is the sweet spot. Long essays look defensive; one-liners look lazy.
  • Never argue publicly. Even if the reviewer is wrong, take the high road. Offer to resolve it offline.
  • Thank every reviewer. Yes, even the 1-star ones. Gratitude for their time is always appropriate.
